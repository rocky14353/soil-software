Old Software – Disadvantages
• Single product recommendation
• Only N,P,K and pH, EC and OC are
analysed
• Only in English
• Reports have to come from Vizag lab and
translated in regional language

New software- Features
• Web enabled software
• Single and multi product recommendation
• Macro and Micro nutrients
recommendation including Sulphur and B
• Lot of MIS reports can be generated

New software- Features
• Reports can be generated in English and
regional languages
• Reports can be generated at respective
branches
• Very user friendly – selection from drop
down menu-type

New software
• Specific information sheet duly filled in must go
along with soil sample
• After Sample analysis- data will be keyed in by
LAB staff
• Branch will receive the (computer generated )
analysed sample numbers on daily basis
• Concerned AM or MO generate the farmer
recommendation sheet (using pre printed
stationary) in Regional language
• Report will be handed over to concerned farmer
immediately

Check List for Soil Analysis in Van
• Chemical Solutions
• pH Buffers- Expiry
• Instruments calibration
• If necessary call ELICO Engineer
• Filter papers & other chemicals
• Soil information sheets

Check list for plant samples
• Proper filling of information sheet
• Tick marking the required gromor grade or
combination of grades
• Required nutrient analysis- NPKS or NPKS +
Zn,B
• LR number, number of samples, transport name
shouldbe informed to Dr. Nilkamal and copy to
HO
• In a single bag not more than 50 soil samples
• If leaf samples not more than 50 samples at a
time.
