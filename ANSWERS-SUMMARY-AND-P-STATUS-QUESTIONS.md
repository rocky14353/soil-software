## ✅ Confirmed Answers

### Bag Sizes:
- **Urea**: 45 kg per bag
- **All other fertilizers**: 50 kg per bag

### Q1: Rounding Bag Steps
**Answer**: Use both (2.5 kg and 5 kg steps), choose whichever gives more precision

### Q2: Midpoint Calculation
**Answer**: ✅ CORRECT
- Formula: (lower + upper) ÷ 2
- Example: 23.7 kg → Options: 22.5 and 25 → Midpoint: 23.75 → Round DOWN to 22.5 kg

### Q3: K Threshold at Exactly 35%
**Answer**: At exactly 35%, **DON'T add MOP** (same as 35-40% rule)
